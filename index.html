Summer Diet Plan Guide

After months of tinkering with my macros this is my diet plan that has helped me lose 20 pounds and go from 230 lbs to 210 lbs in the past 3 months! The macronutriets of this diet consists of...

  • Carbs: 25%/~144grams
  • Protein: 40%/~230grams
  • Fat: 35%/~89grams
  • Calories: 2296

If your maintenance calories is around 2400 calories try this diet out for an easy to manage calorie deficit plan!
